What I do and How it helps

As a UX researcher and product designer, my multidisciplinary background allows me to approach challenges holistically. I can empathize with users, conduct thorough research, and design solutions that not only meet user expectations but also align with business objectives.

My formal education includes Human-Computer Interaction, Anthropology, and Business Strategy. This combination of skills inspires me to create impactful and strategically successful user experiences.

Human-Computer Interaction

Study and design of the interaction between people and digital technologies to create effective, user-friendly, and seamless user experiences.

Anthropology

Examining the social, cultural, and ethical implications of technological advancements within societies.

Business Strategy

Deliberate planning and alignment of organizational goals to optimize user experiences and leverage technological advancements for competitive advantage and sustained success.

The study of anthropology enriches my UX design approach by providing insights into diverse cultural perspectives, allowing me to create designs that resonate with a wide range of users. Understanding cultural nuances is crucial for developing inclusive and user-centric interfaces.

Additionally, human-computer interaction knowledge enhances my ability to craft interfaces that align with user behaviors and expectations, ensuring a seamless and intuitive user experience. The synergy between anthropology and HCI inspires me to create designs that funtion well and consider the cultural context, resulting in globally relevant user experiences.

In my problem-solving endeavors, I draw inspiration from the scientific method, a methodology deeply ingrained from my experience in research. This entails a commitment to systematic investigation and data-driven analysis. My interdisciplinary background, encompassing anthropology and operations/strategic management, further refines this approach.

Anthropology enriches the problem-framing process with a comprehensive understanding of user behaviors within diverse cultural landscapes. Concurrently, operations and strategic management provide a strategic lens, ensuring solutions not only address challenges effectively but also align seamlessly with overarching organizational objectives.
