T-Shaped Designer

My broad educational experience has allowed me to become what Tim Brown, CEO of IDEO, calls a T-shaped designer. My background in anthropology, human-computer interaction, and business allows me to approach design prompts and issues from many different angles and communicate with developers, researchers, and designers alike to better understand their needs.

My specialization in anthropology gives me a deep understanding of human behavior, culture, and social dynamics, which helps me cultivate thorough and endearing results in user research and testing. This, along with my experience in science writing and biology lab research, allows me to take dense or complicated findings and transform them into usable information for design solutions.
