Project Overview

The goal of this project was to improve the user flow for users creating an account and ICEBREKR profile. I collaborated with the Nashville Entrepreneur Center and the CEO of ICEBREKR to help revise their onboarding flow and improve the process.

These suggestion presented and implemented orginate from the Nashville Entrepreneur Center UX event, in which local startups reach out to UX researchers and designers for pro bono advice.

IceBrekr User Flow

Process improvement was the driving force behind this project. The CEO was having issues with the onboarding process, leading to a low enrollemt of new users.

The primary issue on this first page was that users were confused about the types of keywords that they could use. Participants wanted to know how to seperate different keywords (ex. using commas or autofill/suggested options).

The Solution:

Adding suggested terms and changing the search bar to allow for autofill suggestions.

The colored border of the suggested terms demonstrates the seperation of terms that users were previously confused about.

Additionally, the autofill suggestions in the search box would help users get a better understanding of what terms they could use.
