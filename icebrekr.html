Project Overview

In this project I collaborated with the Nashville Entrepreneur Center and the CEO of ICEBREKR to help revise their onboarding flow and improve the process. The goal of this project was to make the app less confusing and more usable, as well as update its slightly dated look. These suggestion presented and implemented orginate from the Nashville Entrepreneur Center UX event, in which local startups reach out to UX researchers and designers for pro bono advice.

Primary Roll

This was a self-led project where I used skills such as process improvement and business research to inform my designs, create low-fidelity mock-ups, test prototypes, and create a final product.

Strengthened Skills

Figma, industry research, time management, process improvement, collaboration.
