IceBrekr UX Redesign

Redesigning IceBrekr's app to improve the apps user flow, helping users easily connect and network with others.
In this case study I address issues such as high rates of incomplete sign ups and general confusion about the capabilities of the application.

Skill set: User Stories, Process Improvement, User Personas, UX Principles

NES Redesign

This project was all about little adjustments to make their website feel more modern and cohesive. During this process I handled small detail oriented issues, like spacing and formatting inconsistencies, and larger overarching issues like overwhelming cognitive loads.

Smart Toilet App

In this personal project, guided by Heather Snyder Quinn (designer and design futurist), I created an interface intended to be used with a smart toilet. This semi-spectulative technology project gave me the opportunity to use my medical anthropology knowledge to create an app where users can track their health and log their personal data securely.

Skill set: UX Research, UX Design, Market Research
