Project Overview

In this project I worked with Northeast Stars Montessori staff to redesign their website. While the original website was informative there were issues with the information architecture and overall design that made the website's information challenging and overwhelming to digest.

I did an extensive redesign of the entire website, upwards of 15 different pages. For the sake of concision I have included 8 of these pages below.

Primary Roll

Industry research and Website Redesign. I had previous experience redesign websites from my Human-Centered Design course at Wash U. During that time I learned how to analyze industry standards for different types of websites and emulate those designs (Following the Heuristic UX Law "Jakob's Law")

Strengthened Skills

Understanding of UX principles, project strategy and organization, information analysis and synthesis informing design, collaboration with clients

Project Timeline

One Week - 4 hours to write and revise a brief with the NES staff, 4 days to complete a redesign, 1 day to consult and revise the redesign with the NES staff.
