Overview


This is the final project I completed for my Business Strategy course. This course and project taught me how to analyze a business's market position, market trends, and competitor's strategic position.

I worked on a team with three other students and the project in total took approximately a month. We had multiple meetings about the quality of our strategy and how to improve it and went through many iterations of the final product you are about to view.

My primary role in this project was researching the department store market and Nordstrom's history and analyzing and synthesizing data about the industry and company. The strategy recommendation was a group effort, which took extensive communication and collaboration.
